Effects of Video Games:

The Positive side:
  • Problem Solving Skills
  • Hand and Eye Coordination
  • Memory
  • Concentration
  • Teamwork
  • Social Interaction
  • Self-expression
  • Cognitive Flexibility
  • Brain Stimulation
  • Enhanced Pro-Social Behaviors
  • Decision-making
  • Enhanced Ability to Multi-task
The Negative side:
  • Anxiety
  • Aggression
  • Depression
  • Obesity
  • Muscle Atrophy
  • Dopamine Addiction
  • Social Disconnection
  • Sleep Disorders
  • Eye Strain
  • Repetitive Strain Injury
  • Difficulties with Work or School
  • Emotional Numbing
